CEO Ethiopia

CEO Ethiopia’s vision is for a world resplendent with big, bold
business ideas. We are here to create and support inventors,
entrepreneurs, and future business leaders.

WHO WE ARE

CEO Ethiopia upskills high-potential university students, help them with a proof of concept for a business they have, connect them with opportunities at the world’s top tech companies, and create digital solutions to urgent problems in their home countries together. The program is free for students, making the opportunity available for youth who have talent but lack the means to use it.

Our Belief

The world is yet to see that talent is everywhere, but the opportunity is not. That is why we want to provide quality education in underdeveloped areas.

Network

We connect youth entrepreneurs to larger networks through mentors who act as role models to demonstrate models of success and help them examine their business plans and ideas, as well as clearly define their goals and obligations.

Internships

Students who passed the interviews get 1-month or a 3-month internships to gain experience in building scalable products that are commonly used around the world.
